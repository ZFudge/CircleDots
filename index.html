Controls

Dots

I: Counter-clockwise
O: Randomize rotation
P: Clockwise
G: Vortex / Criss-X
H: Clear
D: Inward / Outward
F: Slow / Regular

Global

1-9: Images
Z: Stop/Go
X: Reset
C: Solid

Rectangles

W: On / Off
Q: Oscillate / Pass
A: Vertical / Horizontal
J: Left / Up
K: Randomize direction
L: Right / Down
